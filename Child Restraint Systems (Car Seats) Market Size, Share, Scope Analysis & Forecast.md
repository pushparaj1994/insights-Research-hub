Global Child Restraint Systems (Car Seats) Market by Application
The Global Child Restraint Systems (Car Seats) Market is segmented based on various applications that cater to different stages of a child's growth. The primary categories include infant car seats, convertible car seats, booster seats, and combination seats. Infant car seats are designed for newborns and infants up to approximately 1 year old, offering rear-facing installation to provide optimal protection. Convertible car seats can be used both rear-facing and forward-facing, accommodating children as they grow from infancy through toddlerhood. Booster seats are intended for older children who have outgrown their forward-facing car seats but are not yet large enough to use standard seat belts safely.
