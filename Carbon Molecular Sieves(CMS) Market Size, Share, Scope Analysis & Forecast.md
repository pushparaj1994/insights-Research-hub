The global Carbon Molecular Sieves (CMS) market is experiencing significant growth across various applications, primarily driven by the increasing demand for efficient separation technologies. In the industrial sector, CMS is extensively utilized for nitrogen generation in applications such as oil and gas, power generation, and chemical manufacturing. The ability of CMS to selectively separate nitrogen from air makes it indispensable in processes requiring high-purity nitrogen. This has led to its widespread adoption in industries where nitrogen is used as an inert gas to prevent oxidation or as a blanketing gas to protect sensitive materials. Additionally, CMS is crucial in the production of nitrogen-rich gases for food packaging and preservation, where its high performance and reliability contribute to extended shelf life and maintained product quality.

Another key application of CMS is in the field of air separation for medical and healthcare purposes.
